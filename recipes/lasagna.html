Lasagna

Description: Lasagna is an Italian dish made of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with fillings such as ragù (meat sauce), béchamel sauce, cheeses (like ricotta, mozzarella, or Parmesan), and vegetables, then baked until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Prepare dish
  3. Layer lasagna
  4. Bake and cool
